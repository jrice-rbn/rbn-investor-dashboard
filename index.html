Revenue Recognition Note

Accounting

Why P&L Revenue Differs from Full Referral Fees

  • The P&L shows recognized revenue (net of rewards points liability)
  • For investor purposes, the full referral fee is the relevant top-line revenue figure
  • ~2/3 of each referral fee is held as reward points liability on the balance sheet
  • ~1/3 of each referral fee is recognized as revenue in the period

Example: On a $12,350 referral fee — Full fee (investor view): $12,350 | Points liability (~2/3): ~$8,233 | Recognized on P&L (~1/3): ~$4,117

2025 Comparison: P&L recognized revenue: $334,666 (Jan-Nov) vs. Full referral fees: $945,834 (full year)
